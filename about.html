About

EpicJohn Studios is a Game Development Team formed by CEO and Founder John Fiore in the Summer of 2022, alongside Kelby Tarver and Nick Armes. The team is known for creating titles for PC and VR consoles, and wishes to release some for other consoles such as Microsoft Xbox, and Sony Playstation, and possibly even the Nintendo Switch.

The Team
